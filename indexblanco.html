About Me

My name is Esteban and I am a freelance web developer who specializes in creating dynamic and beautiful web pages. I have been in the field for nearly 7 years, and have been loving every minute of it. I am a entrepreneur, designer, developer, marketer and overall thinker.

I am a self-taught guy who has been a designer and developer for a along time now. What started as an inquisitive hobby designing custom Hi5 and MySpace pages for my peers soon progressed into a passion and casual hustle. Today, I enjoy breaking the rules of design and creating awesome things while collaborating with digitally native creatives and brands to develop their brands beyond a logo.

Like most people, I got into Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) by accident. I enter to a online curse hoping to learn about Wordpress but I enroll into a SEO curse. One year later, I became an SEO Consultant and the rest is history.
